Alexei Morozov

Photographer & Visual Artist

Based between Moscow and the Ural Mountains, I have spent over a decade chasing light across continents — from the frozen tundra of Siberia to the sun-scorched coastlines of southern Europe.

My work explores the quiet tension between landscape and human presence. I am drawn to moments of transition: dawn before the world wakes, the last colour before a storm, the stillness after snowfall.

I shoot primarily on medium-format digital and occasionally on large-format film when a scene demands permanence. Every image is processed by hand — no presets, no shortcuts.
